Courses

A list of courses I've taken at Berkeley, with descriptions.

Design

  • ME 130- Design of Planar Machinery (Current, Fall 2020):
    • Synthesis/analysis/simulation of planar machines. Includes designing linkages, reciprocating machines, cams, and flywheels.
  • ME 135- Design of Microprocessor Based Mechanical Systems (Spring 2020):
    • Designing systems that use microprocessors to control machine behavior. Learned LabVIEW for state machine control. (under projects)
  • E 26- 3D Modeling for Design (Spring 2019):
    • Learning SolidWorks. Final project involved modeling and testing a wind turbine. (under projects)
  • E 25- Visualization for Design (Fall 2018):
    • Sketching and visualizing 3D objects. Learned AutoCAD to create 2D sketches.

Manufacturing

  • ME 122- Processing of Materials in Manufacturing (Spring 2020):
    • Selection and processing of materials for different manufacturing applications. (under projects)
  • ME 108- Mechanical Behavior of Engineering Materials (Spring 2020):
    • In-depth examination and testing of material behavior under different conditions.
  • E 27- Intro Manufacturing and Tolerancing (Spring 2020):
    • Basics of manufacturing processes and geometric dimensioning and tolerancing. (under projects)
  • ME C85- Statics and Mechanics of Materials (Spring 2019):
    • Studying stress/strain, deformation, different forms of loading, and static equilibrium for varying materials.

Electronics/Programming

  • EECS 106A- Introduction to Robotics (Current, Fall 2020):
    • Control/dynamics of robotic manipulators, all programmed during simulation labs. Intro to computer vision, sensing, motion detection.
  • ME 132- Dynamic Systems and Feedback (Current, Fall 2020):
    • Control/dynamics of robotic manipulators, all programmed during simulation labs. Intro to computer vision, sensing, motion detection.
  • EECS 16B- Design of Information Devices and Systems II (Fall 2019):
    • Exploring signal processing, feedback control systems, filtering, and advanced circuit design. Applications of linear algebra for data processing and classification. (under projects)
  • CS 61B- Data Structures (Fall 2019):
    • Fundamental data structures. Intro to algorithms and software engineering. (under projects)
  • EECS 16A- Design of Information Devices and Systems I (Spring 2019):
    • Introduction to circuit design and data processing.
  • E 7- Intro to Programming for Engineers (Fall 2018):
    • Programming and plotting in MATLAB.

Fundamentals

  • ME 104 Engineering Mechanics (Current, Fall 2020):
    • Dynamics of particles and rigid bodies.
  • Math 54- Linear Algebra and Differential Equations (Fall 2019):
    • Fundamentals of linear algebra. Methods for solving ODEs and PDEs.
  • Physics 7B- Physics for Scientists and Engineers II (Summer 2019):
    • Heat, electricity, magnetism.
  • Math 53- Multivariable calculus. (Summer 2019):
    • Integration and differentiation with multiple variables. Vector calculus.
  • Physics 7A- Physics for Scientists and Engineers I (Spring 2019):
    • Mechanics and wave motion.

Miscellaneous

  • UGBA 198- Intro to Product Management (Fall 2019):
    • Basics of a product management role. Learning elements of a good product spec.
